Who We Are

Siva Overseas is a trusted education loan consultancy committed to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. With tie-ups across 20+ financial institutions, we provide transparent and efficient guidance for both secured and unsecured loans.

We assist students throughout the loan process—from documentation to final disbursal—and offer personalized support to ensure a hassle-free experience.

Whether you’re heading to the US, UK, Canada, or any other destination, our expert team ensures you find the right funding at the best possible terms.
